## Notes

- All resolutions at a general meeting are decided on a show of hands unless a poll is demanded in accordance with the company's constitution.
- A poll may be demanded by the chair, by at least two members present and entitled to vote, or by a member or members holding at least 5% of the votes that may be cast on the resolution.
- Special resolutions require at least 28 days' notice and 75% of votes cast in favour.
- Proxies must be lodged at least 48 hours before the meeting.
