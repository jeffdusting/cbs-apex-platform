Dear David,

Re: Response to Condition of Participation - RFP No. WS5364262133

Thank you for your email regarding the Condition of Participation for the Asset Management Support Services RFP (WS5364262133). We confirm that our proposal, submitted on 1st December 2025, addresses this condition in full.

As a summary of our compliance, our submission details extensive experience across the required areas of expertise. This includes developing video-only tolling models and whole-of-life costings for the WestConnex, Sydney Harbour Tunnel, and M6 projects; leading technical and commercial due diligence for these same assets; and creating long-term, 30-year asset management models and lifecycle costings. Furthermore, our experience extends to asset commissioning and transition on major projects such as the WestConnex Motorways (M4, M5, and M8), ensuring comprehensive readiness from construction to operation and managing end-of-contract transitions.

To further assist the project team with their evaluation, we have attached a detailed appendix. This document maps each specific Condition of Participation to the precise locations within our proposal where our compliance and experience are detailed, providing:

The location of the formal compliance statement in our proposal.

The specific sections where each condition is addressed in detail.

A summary of how each condition has been met, including relevant project experience.

We trust this provides the necessary clarity for your evaluation. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require any further information.
