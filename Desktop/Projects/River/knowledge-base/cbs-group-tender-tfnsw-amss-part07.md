## Executive Summary FINAL.docx

*File: `Working finals/Executive Summary FINAL.docx`*

A Partnership of Certainty, Value, and Commitment

On behalf of the entire CBS Group, I am delighted to present our proposal to continue our valued partnership with Transport for NSW (TfNSW). 

Over the past 18 months, we have been privileged to work alongside your Asset Management team on some of the state’s most significant infrastructure programs. The trust you have placed in us is a responsibility we take very seriously. 

This proposal is our commitment to repaying that trust by delivering what we believe no other partner can: the certainty of proven performance, the security of zero transition risk, and a genuine partnership dedicated to delivering value to the people of NSW.

Our proposal is built on a foundation of certainty. In a landscape often defined by complexity and unpredictability, CBS Group offers a proven, low-risk path to achieving TfNSW’s strategic asset management objectives. 

Our track record with TfNSW speaks for itself. We have delivered over $1.049 billion in validated savings across your programs through the rigorous application of our proprietary CAPITAL Framework. This is not a theoretical model but a proven engine for value creation, demonstrated on your own assets. Our consistent 97.2% on-time delivery rate and a 4.7/5.0 client satisfaction score reflect the strength of our collaborative relationship and our deep understanding of your operational and strategic needs. These are the tangible outcomes of our mutual partnership.

Continuity is a powerful strategic advantage. Our proposal eliminates the significant risks, costs, and delays associated with onboarding a new provider. We offer TfNSW the certainty of immediate, uninterrupted productivity from Day One by proposing a core team of seven senior specialists, most of whom who are already delivering for you. This team, with over 175 years of combined experience, possesses an invaluable understanding of your assets, governance, and priorities. By choosing CBS Group, you avoid a typical 7-12 month learning curve and the associated productivity dip.

As a 100% Australian-owned SME, our success is intrinsically linked to the prosperity of NSW. We offer an agile, responsive service model that provides direct access to senior leadership, ensuring greater efficiency and value. Our commitment extends beyond the project to creating lasting social and economic benefits for the people of NSW, demonstrated through our First Nations Engineering Internship & Innovation Fund and our dedication to 95%+ local content.

In summary, CBS Group offers TfNSW a partnership built on a foundation of trust and proven results. We provide the certainty of an expert team that is already performing, a methodology that has already delivered exceptional value, and a shared commitment to the future success of Transport for NSW. We look forward to the opportunity to continue this successful journey with you.

CBS Group's unique market position ensures our recommendations serve only the State's best interests, free from the conflicts inherent in traditional advisory options.

In procuring strategic asset management advice, TfNSW must navigate three distinct risks:

Analytical capability without operational insight — rigorous analysis undermined by operations-phase blind spots, defaults to "lazy precedent," and innovation constrained by professional indemnity exposure.

Operational experience compromised by commercial interest — delivery knowledge shaped to suit the adviser's own capabilities or downstream ambitions, creating conflicts that undermine procurement integrity.

Commercial expertise without technical depth — financial and transactional structuring that lacks deep asset management knowledge, relying on fragmented specialist inputs that dilute accountability and create coordination overhead.

CBS threads this needle. Our team, structure, and independence allow us to combine top-tier analytical calibre with genuine operational experience—free from these competing tensions. With no downstream delivery contracts or transaction fees influencing our advice, we challenge established norms and drive innovation aligned solely with best value for the State.

With CBS, TfNSW has absolute certainty: impartial advice, rigorous analysis, and a singular focus on your success.

Sarah Taylor
Partner Strategy and Technology
CBS Group

December 1, 2025
