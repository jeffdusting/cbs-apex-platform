Re: Environmental Energy Finance Master Services Agreement dated 8 March 2021 (“Agreement”) — Response to Reference DOWRAI-LTTR-21450

Dear Ms Bond,

1.	CBS Group refers to your letter dated 30 March 2026 regarding the Agreement between Downer EDI Rail Pty Ltd (“Downer”) and CBS Group Pty Ltd (“CBS Group”). We address each matter raised below.

Outstanding Invoices — Certificate Sale Notices #16 and #17

2.	CBS Group confirms that payment of the Downer Fee of $528,605 (including GST) for use of Downer information to generate, accredit, and trade 30,000 Energy Savings Certificates in respect of Certificate Sale Notices #16 and #17 has been made and funds have cleared. The delay was caused by an internal accounting system error that held Downer’s invoices in draft status. CBS Group apologises for the administrative inconvenience and has implemented controls to prevent recurrence.

3.	We note that Downer’s characterisation of “repeated failures” to remit payment in a timely manner does not reflect the full history of this Agreement. CBS Group’s own records demonstrate that between September 2021 and June 2022, Downer held four CBS Group invoices unpaid for periods of up to 299 days, settling them in a single batch payment on 28 June 2022. Earlier in the Agreement, Downer’s delay in issuing invoices to CBS Group for a period exceeding six months created GST reporting complications for CBS Group. Both parties have experienced administrative imperfections over the life of this Agreement, and CBS Group does not consider that these are reflective of anything other than operational issues on both sides.

Reimbursement of Fees for Services

4.	CBS Group acknowledges the reimbursement obligation under Appendix C of the Agreement upon first Certificate Sale, and confirms that it agrees with the quantum of $220,000 (ex GST) as set out in Downer’s Fee Reimbursement Schedule dated 26 March 2026.

5.	CBS Group notes one matter requiring resolution before payment can proceed. Under clause 11 of the Agreement, a tax invoice is a precondition to payment of any amount including GST. To date, CBS Group has not received a tax invoice from Downer for the reimbursement amount. The Appendix C Fee Reimbursement Schedule attached to your letter does not constitute a compliant tax invoice. CBS Group requests that Downer issue a valid tax invoice for the claimed amount so that payment can be processed.

6.	We further note that the first Certificate Sale occurred approximately 10 months ago, and this is the first formal claim for the reimbursement amount accompanied by supporting documentation. Downer’s reference to an email of 28 January 2026 is noted, however no tax invoice accompanied that correspondence.

7.	CBS Group also notes that it continues to incur material costs in generating, accrediting, and trading the remaining certificates — including scheme administration, auditor fees, brokerage, and market management. These are costs borne solely by CBS Group under the Agreement. CBS Group proposes that the resolution of the reimbursement be addressed in the context of the broader discussion on the sales strategy for the remaining certificate inventory, with the objective of ensuring an orderly and commercially sound approach to the sale of the remaining certificates. CBS Group will process the reimbursement payment within 10 Business Days of receipt of a valid tax invoice, subject to agreement on the path forward.

Audit

8.	CBS Group notes Downer’s notification of intent to conduct an audit pursuant to clause 22.3 of the Agreement.

9.	With respect, CBS Group does not accept that the grounds stated in your letter support the exercise of audit rights under clause 22.3. That clause provides for audit in two circumstances only:

(a)	to verify CBS Group’s compliance with clause 21 of the Agreement (which addresses taxes, employee benefits and workers’ obligations — matters unrelated to the payment of the Downer Fee following certificate sales); or

(b)	in relation to a Probity Event (or suspected Probity Event) as defined in the Agreement.

10.	An administrative delay in processing invoices caused by an accounting system error does not constitute a Probity Event, nor does it engage clause 21. Accordingly, the contractual prerequisites for the exercise of audit rights have not been established.

11.	Notwithstanding the above, CBS Group values transparency and the ongoing commercial relationship. CBS Group is willing to provide Downer with a full reconciliation of all Certificate sales, CBS Group’s entitlements, the Downer Fee for each sale, and payment dates on a voluntary basis. We consider this offers Downer the substantive assurance it seeks without the need to invoke audit provisions on grounds that do not meet the contractual threshold.

Remaining Certificates

12.	CBS Group notes Downer’s request for a status update on the remaining balance of Certificates. Downer’s letter references the position as at Sale Notice #17 of 6 February 2026 (63,510 for Waratah and 193,969 for SGT). CBS Group advises that this position is no longer current. As at Sale Notice #020 dated 16 March 2026, the remaining certificate balance is 23,510 for Waratah and 193,969 for SGT (total: 217,479). CBS Group has continued to actively trade certificates in the period since Downer’s reference date.

13.	CBS Group understands that a meeting between CBS Group and Downer’s senior management has been scheduled to discuss the remaining certificate inventory, including market timing, pricing approach, and the orderly sale of the remaining certificates. CBS Group considers that this forum is the appropriate venue to address Downer’s questions regarding the remaining Certificates in a constructive and comprehensive manner, and we look forward to that discussion.

14.	CBS Group acknowledges that Downer’s entitlement to the Downer Fee from the sale of Certificates survives expiry of the Agreement Term, in accordance with clause 16.2, and confirms its continued commitment to completing the sale of the remaining certificates in accordance with the Agreement.

General

15.	CBS Group is committed to fulfilling its obligations under the Agreement and to maintaining a constructive commercial relationship with Downer. We trust this response addresses the matters raised in your letter and look forward to resolving the remaining items through direct engagement.

16.	CBS Group reserves all rights under the Agreement and at law.

Yours sincerely,

Sarah Taylor

Contract Representative

CBS Group Pty Ltd
