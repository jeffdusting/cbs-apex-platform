## RFP Service reqs by phase AI summary.docx

*File: `Superseded/RFP Service reqs by phase AI summary.docx`*

DHS AI summary

Service Delivery requirements by phase

Phase 1 — Discovery & Assessment

Core Requirements

Conduct a systematic assessment of maintenance models, systems, and processes across selected pilot health services.

Review existing engineering maintenance practices to understand variability and maturity.

Undertake a readiness evaluation to determine each service’s capability to implement KPIs, including:

Data availability and quality

Resourcing

Operational constraints

Use stakeholder information provided by the Department to plan targeted engagement.

Supporting Excerpt

“Undertake a systematic assessment of maintenance models, systems, and processes… Assess organisational and operational readiness for KPI implementation…”

Phase 2 — Data Analysis & Benchmarking

Core Requirements

Collect and validate maintenance data from participating health services.

Perform benchmarking analysis to identify:

Strengths

Weaknesses

Gaps

Variations against best practice

Segment health services into maturity cohorts based on:

Asset management capability

Operational capacity

Work with the Department to determine:

The size of the pilot group

Which health services will participate

Supporting Excerpt

“Analyse data to identify sector-wide strengths, weaknesses, and gaps; compare against best practice standards… classify into distinct cohorts according to their asset management maturity…”

Phase 3 — Engagement & Co-Design

Core Requirements

Facilitate structured engagement activities, including:

Workshops

Interviews

Surveys

Capture insights on:

Local challenges

Innovations

Operational constraints

Co-design tailored support options and action plans with stakeholders.

Ensure outputs align with the operational realities of diverse health services.

Supporting Excerpt

“Facilitate workshops, interviews, and surveys… Co-develop tailored support options and action plans with stakeholders…”

Phase 4 — Pilot Implementation

Core Requirements

Develop a detailed pilot plan, including:

Pilot scope

Engagement schedule

Data collection approach

Deliver direct consultation and workshops with pilot health services.

Monitor pilot performance and collect KPI data.

Evaluate pilot effectiveness and document lessons learned.

Supporting Excerpt

“Design and execute a pilot phase… Collect pilot data, evaluate effectiveness, and document lessons learned.”

Phase 5 — Full Implementation & Continuous Improvement

Core Requirements

Refine implementation and action plans based on pilot outcomes.

Develop and deliver a change management strategy, including:

Communications

Engagement

Stakeholder alignment

Establish mechanisms for:

Ongoing monitoring

Evaluation

Continuous improvement

Support sector-wide adoption of the KPI framework.

Supporting Excerpt

“Develop and deliver communication and engagement strategies… Establish mechanisms for ongoing monitoring, evaluation, and continuous improvement.”
