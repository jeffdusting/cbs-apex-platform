| Sample Projects
DEFENCE ASSET MANAGEMENT
Project management, stakeholder engagement, requirements management, tender preparation and evaluation for Defence Joint Project JP2077 for integrated supply, asset and inventory management, maintenance and distribution throughout Defence.  Project included delivery of an enhanced information systems capability for the materiel logistics support processes, and development and implementation of best-practice technologies, maintenance processes and performance metrics.  On a separate project, CBS designed and deployed network sensors to improve the asset management of remote defence assets, including power generators, armoury, and fuel depots.
HEALTH
Gold Coast University Health Services.  Incudes Australia’s newest hospital, a facility designed to provide services for Southern Queensland and act as a regional hub for disaster response.   CBS designed the data analysis tool required to manage outputs and alerts on the more than 500 critical systems (environment, water, gases, power, security, HVAC etc.) that are the basis for medical care.  Similar projects have been delivered at Newcastle University.
GOVERNMENT.  
CBS designed and deployed a sensor network for critical water and sewage assets, monitoring and reporting on asset performance as the basis for predictive maintenance. The program successfully ensured optimal asset performance under peak loads, providing reliable access to services and utilities during the tourist season when visitors increase the population by more than 1m.
MINERALS 
Rio Tinto Limited.  Independent review of solution options for Enterprise LTE (4G) network for rail communications network coverage for pit to port operations for the Rio Tinto Iron Ore Board.
BHP Iron Ore.  Independent technical review of delivery solutions for an Enterprise LTE (4G) rail communications network for pit to port automated haulage.
RAIL
High Capacity Metro Trains (HCMT) Project.  The HCMT Project involves the delivery of 65 next-generation high-capacity metro trains capable of carrying 20 per cent more passengers than any other train on the network. The fleet will be supported by a new train maintenance depot in Pakenham East, a light service facility in Calder Park and simulators for training.  CBS has been engaged to establish and deliver Configuration Management, Systems Engineering and System Integration services for the whole of system solution, addressing operations and control procedures, risk and safety management and providing assurance of adherence to Victorian and Rail Industry Safety and Standards Board (RISSB) products.  CBS’ services has also included Reliability, Availability and Maintainability (RAM) analyses and System Safety Management, which have driven system design, the definition of operations and maintenance procedures, adoption of safety controls and a framework for the condition monitoring of systems.  
Melbourne Metro Rail Systems.  Alliance bid GSM-R rail communications system development, including RF Optimisation and Audit Program, Critical Design review for system integration, and contract negotiations for tunnel coverage design assessments and coordination of design processes.  
Sydney Trains.  Consulted on the Enterprise Asset Management Programme in the areas of advanced asset lifecycle optimization, machine learning systems, and related technologies for complex business processes and optimised decision support for asset management.  
Sydney Trains.  Development of project and program governance policies for the Fleet Maintenance Division, as part of the Fleet Maintenance Division Reform program.  Successful completion and delivery of ISO9001-level process documentation capturing corporate knowledge relating to inter- and intra-divisional day-to-day business processes, including the Annual Works Programme (AWP), major periodic maintenance (MPM) and warranty and out-of-course maintenance (OOC), including interface with related business units e.g., Project Office for project governance, and Engineering in regard to incorporation of condition monitoring data from all relevant systems.  This project is part of the “Fixing the Trains” Ministerial initiative.  Additional value achieved by way of systematic mapping of detailed core business processes in BPMN2, and optimization via reengineering and verification of improvements via statistical simulation, education of the permanent workforce in regard to business analysis tools and techniques (BizAgi, ARIS), and how to apply them in a Six-Sigma and ISO 55000 context, e.g. process validation and simulation.  
EDI Rail.  Scoped and established the Reliability, Availability & Maintainability (RAM) programme to recover operational performance and project delivery issues for the Millennium train fleet.
OTHER
Transurban.  Systems engineering and test and verification services for the delivery of the Central Computer Control System for the CityLink Traffic Control Room, including delivery of air monitoring and ground water management systems.  Definition, design and implementation of a maintenance and real-time condition monitoring data system.  
Eurocat Life Extension.  Project engineering services for this nation-wide replacement of aging, obsolescent Air Traffic Management hardware and porting of software.  The project identified all systems touched by the ATM system, network and data sources and confirmed/updated the existing asset register.  This resulted in a 2 year project to install in parallel a new ATM system whilst keeping the existing one going.  The cutover between old and new was achieved with success.  
Video Analytics.  CBS designed, and project managed the installation of a video analytics system for Australia’s largest 24/7 retailer, 7-Eleven.  Data collection included demography, dwell times, time-of-day, POS and stock data.  Analysis was used as the basis for improved commercial strategies.  CBS is at the forefront of video analytics in transport systems with team members leading the systems design for video analytics in major Intelligent Transport Systems (ITS) projects on Australian motorways. |

Asset Management

CBS Group

Two of Australia’s leading boutique consulting firms, CobaltBlu and Sandwood Consulting, have combined to form CBS Group.  Our combined resources provide access to a large pool of experienced professional personnel. We can effectively match executive, operational, technical and commercial capabilities to your bid and tender requirements.

AM Expertise

Our team comes with an engineering, operations, maintenance and Asset Management background – having worked with infrastructure from design, construction, operations, maintenance and disposal throughout the asset lifecycle.

We have significant experience in developing and implementing asset management systems and are particularly knowledgeable around the new ISO 55000 suite of asset management standards.

Members of our team have been engaged in a broad range of asset management functions including development of asset management plans, business case development, asset lifecycle risk analysis, compliance and asset management audits.

Typical Engagements

CBS Group team members have been engaged in a wide variety of roles to assist our clients meet their asset management objectives – for example, ensuring asset availability and public safety in the most cost effective manner possible over the assets’ lifecycle

We have been engaged to both lead and develop asset management solutions during major infrastructure EOI / Tender stage, drawing on deep experience in current technologies and practices.

We have also been engaged to provide ‘in-service’ asset management leadership, planning and solution development to achieve both enhanced lifecycle efficiencies and, advice on system upgrades, asset augmentation and other asset life elongation and asset transition programs

These activities have been provided over a variety of asset types including major network assets such as roads and utilities, building related assets such as hospitals and other infrastructure types such as tunnels
