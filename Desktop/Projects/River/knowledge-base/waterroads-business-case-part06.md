WATER ROADS HYDROFOIL TECHNOLOGY: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY FOR
  NON-TECHNICAL AUDIENCE
•    For Non-Technical Stakeholders
•    Date: August 2025

OVERVIEW
Water Roads represents a transformative approach to Sydney Harbour transport using advanced hydrofoil
technology that lifts vessels above the water surface for dramatically improved speed, efficiency, and
environmental performance.

This technology, proven in European markets, offers NSW the opportunity to lead globally in sustainable urban
water transport.

KEY PERFORMANCE BENEFITS
Speed and Reliability
•    30+ knots operational speed (55+ km/h) - more than double conventional ferry speeds
•    18-minute Rhodes-Barangaroo journey compared to current 35+ minute services
•    6-minute service frequency enabling metro-style operations
•    99% operational reliability with predictable travel times regardless of harbour traffic


Environmental Leadership
•    Zero direct emissions through full electric propulsion
•    85% energy reduction compared to conventional diesel ferries
•    95% wake reduction protecting Sydney Harbour shorelines and marine life
•    Elimination of carcinogenic diesel emissions benefiting 5,000+ daily passengers


Economic Efficiency
•    Lower operational costs through reduced energy consumption and maintenance
•    Faster passenger throughput, enabling higher revenue per vessel
•    Minimal infrastructure requirements utilising existing waterways
•    24% return on investment with proven commercial viability

TECHNOLOGY PARTNERSHIP
Water Roads leverages partnerships with two global technology leaders:

•    Candela (Sweden) - P-12 Shuttle with 30-passenger capacity, extensively proven in European
     operations with thousands of operational hours demonstrating reliability and passenger
     satisfaction.
•    Artemis Technologies (UK) - EF-12 Transporter with 12-passenger capacity, bringing America's
     Cup racing technology to commercial transport for enhanced operational flexibility.

VALIDATION PROGRAM
Before full deployment, Water Roads will implement a comprehensive validation program:

•    Technology Demonstration - Pilot vessels operating in Sydney Harbour conditions to validate
     performance claims and build stakeholder confidence.
•    Regulatory Approval - Working closely with the Australian Maritime Safety Authority and NSW
     Maritime to establish operational frameworks and safety compliance.
•    Stakeholder Engagement - Demonstration events for government, industry, and community
     stakeholders to experience the technology firsthand.
•    Operational Readiness - Crew training, maintenance procedures, and integration with existing
     transport networks.

STRATEGIC ADVANTAGES
•    First-Mover Opportunity: NSW can establish global leadership in sustainable harbour transport,
     creating export opportunities and international recognition.
•    Proven Technology: Hydrofoil vessels are operating successfully in Stockholm, Helsinki, and
     other international markets, reducing implementation risk.
•    Scalable Solution: The initial Rhodes-Barangaroo service lays the foundation for a harbour-wide
     network expansion, serving multiple routes and destinations.
•    Innovation Showcase: Demonstrates Australian capability in adopting and implementing
     cutting-edge clean technology solutions.

RISK MITIGATION
•    Technology Risk: Comprehensive testing and validation program ensures performance meets all
     specifications before passenger operations commence.
•    Operational Risk: Extensive crew training and maintenance programs based on international
     best practices ensure safe and reliable operations.
•    Regulatory Risk: Proactive engagement with maritime authorities ensures compliance with all
     safety and environmental requirements.
•    Market Risk: Conservative demand projections and flexible service patterns enable adaptation
     to passenger demand variations.

IMPLEMENTATION TIMELINE
•    Months 1-2: Vessel procurement and regulatory consultation initiation
•    Months 3-4: Performance testing and stakeholder demonstrations
•    Months 5-6: Regulatory approvals and operational readiness validation

Success in Phase 0 enables progression to full-scale implementation with confidence in technology
performance, regulatory compliance, and stakeholder support.
