To claim the unit, the Emissions Reduction Fund Project Report and Crediting Application form must be completed and submitted for the reporting period. These are assessed within 90 days once received.

Once the claim for ACCUs is approved, an abatement statement from the Clean Energy Regulator will be received advising how many ACCUs your project is entitled to receive for the reporting period and the ACCUs will be transferred into your ANREU account.
