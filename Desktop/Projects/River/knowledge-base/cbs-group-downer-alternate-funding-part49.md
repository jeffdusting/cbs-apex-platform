 BACKGROUND & Acknowledgements
      A. Downer carries on a business that at times involves advice and services on the procurement,
         design, installation, servicing and/or maintenance of efficient end-user equipment by their
         Customers which may be entitled for the creation of Environmental Products (Certificates)
         under the Environmental Schemes.

      B. Downer and CBS Group have agreed to partner in the novel application of Environmental
         Schemes to mobile plant. A particular application of this partnership is the pursuit of Energy
         Saving Certificates from the NSW Energy Savings Scheme resulting from modifications to the
         Sydney Trains SGT and Waratah fleets maintained by Downer.

      C. Downer wishes to engage CBS Group to provide the services required to generate value from
         Energy Savings Certificates under the NSW Energy Savings Scheme, and CBS Group has
         agreed to provide the Services on the terms set out in this Agreement. CBS Group will work
         with Downer to collect evidence. Downer will receive payments based on the successful
         registration of certificates for the nominated projects (Scheme Activities)

      D. CBS Group has represented to Downer that it has the skill, experience, personnel, material
         and capacity to supply the Services in accordance with this Agreement.
