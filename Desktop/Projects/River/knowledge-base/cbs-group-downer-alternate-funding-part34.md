Sydney Trains has nominated NGE as the Energy Saver under section 5.2(b) of the Rule in relation to the implementation of the HVAC Software Update, by executing the Energy Saver Nomination Form; and

The Supplier agrees to implement the Amended Variation Order as soon as reasonably practicable after the conditions set out in paragraphs (b)(i)-(iii) have been satisfied.

Implementation of the ESS Variation Proposal

Supplier obligations

The Supplier agrees to implement the ESS Variation Proposal in accordance with the terms of this Deed and the Act, Regulation and Rule.

In implementing the ESS Variation Proposal, the Supplier must:

appoint:

NGE as the Accredited Certificate Provider;

an M&V Professional; and

a Scheme Auditor;

procure that NGE:

applies to the Scheme Administrator as soon as reasonably practicable after the Effective Date to become accredited as a certificate provider in relation to the implementation of the HVAC Software Update as a Recognised Energy Saving Activity;

creates Energy Savings Certificates in respect of the energy savings arising from the implementation of the HVAC Software Update (HVAC Energy Savings Certificates) promptly after the energy savings occur and in any event within the timeframe prescribed by the Act, Regulation or Rule (as applicable), and maximises the number of HVAC Energy Savings Certificates created;

agrees to not create any Energy Savings Certificates in respect of the implementation of the HVAC Software Update, other than pursuant to the terms of this Deed;

applies to the Scheme Administrator for registration of the creation of the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates promptly after the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates are created;

sells the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates promptly after the registration of the creation of the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates, and maximises the price received for the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates; 

applies to the Scheme Administrator for a transfer of the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates to the relevant purchaser; and

transfers the Net ESC Sale Proceeds from the sale of the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates to the Supplier within 5 Business Days after receiving the ESC Sale Proceeds;

provide any information to NGE, the M&V Professional, the Scheme Auditor and the Scheme Administrator, as each may require;

establish a Baseline Energy Model and Operating Energy Model (as applicable)  in accordance with the requirements in section 7A of the Rule, and have this approved by the M&V Professional in accordance with the Rule;

collect all necessary data and information to support the verification of performance;

verify performance in accordance with the processes and requirements set out in the Rule;

compile any supporting evidence that may be required in accordance with the Rule; and

obtain audit confirmation of the energy savings,

(the ESS Activities).

The Supplier agrees to provide:

information regarding the ESS Activities and the implementation of the Amended Variation Order and ESS Variation Proposal; and

subject to the Supplier obtaining Sydney Trains' prior written consent, reasonable access to the SGT Fleet (as the implementation site for the energy savings activity),

to a representative of the ESS or a member of the ESS Audit Services Panel upon request by them.

Appointed parties

The Supplier must use all reasonable endeavours to procure that NGE, the M&V Professional and the Scheme Auditor do all things necessary (including executing agreements and documents) to implement and give full effect to the ESS Variation Proposal.

The Supplier will be responsible and liable for:

the performance of NGE's, the M&V Professional's and the Scheme Auditor's activities; and

the fees and other costs of NGE, the M&V Professional and the Scheme Auditor (including any fees or costs payable to the Scheme Administrator in connection with an application for accreditation or the registration of the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates),

in connection with the implementation of the ESS Variation Proposal.

The Supplier warrants that NGE, the M&V Professional and the Scheme Auditor appointed by the Supplier are reputable, and have sufficient experience, expertise and ability to perform the relevant activities contemplated by this Deed and under the Act, Regulation and Rule.

Sydney Trains obligation to cooperate

Sydney Trains must use all reasonable endeavours to cooperate with and provide such reasonable assistance as is necessary to support the Supplier in implementing the ESS Variation Proposal.

Energy Saver nomination

The parties acknowledge it is intended that, in relation to the implementation of the HVAC Software Update, Sydney Trains will be:

the Purchaser, as defined in section 10.1 of the Rule, being the person who purchases the goods or services that enable the relevant energy savings to be made; and

the Energy Saver under sections 5.2(a) and 7A.18 of the Rule, being the person defined as the Energy Saver in the Project Impact Assessment with Measurement and Verification Method (PIAM&V Method).

The Supplier agrees to provide any information necessary to give effect to paragraph (a), including in relation to its arrangements with Sydney Trains and the implementation of the Amended Variation Order and the ESS Variation Proposal, upon request by Sydney Trains, a representative of the ESS or a member of the ESS Audit Services Panel.

As the person defined as the Energy Saver in the PIAM&V Method, Sydney Trains must nominate NGE as the Energy Saver under section 5.2(b) of the Rule, in relation to the implementation of the HVAC Software Update, by executing the Energy Saver Nomination Form.

By executing the Energy Saver Nomination Form, Sydney Trains entitles NGE, as the Accredited Certificate Provider, to:

create and own all energy savings arising from the implementation of the HVAC Software Update; and

create and register the HVAC Energy Savings Certificates,

until such time as the nomination is revoked.

The nomination of NGE as the Energy Saver:

takes effect from the date the Energy Saver Nomination Form is signed by Sydney Trains and remains in force until the nomination is revoked by Sydney Trains; and

may be revoked by Sydney Trains at any time.

Calculation of energy savings

The parties acknowledge that the energy savings arising from the implementation of the HVAC Software Update will be calculated using the PIAM&V Method under the Rule.

ESC Sale Proceeds

Distribution of ESC Sale Proceeds

The Supplier will be responsible and liable for the recovery of the Net ESC Sale Proceeds from NGE.

Within 2 Business Days after the Payment Trigger Date, the Supplier will pay to Sydney Trains the Sydney Trains Share of Proceeds.

Liability of the Supplier

The Supplier will always be solely liable for the costs of implementing the ESS Variation Proposal, including the ACP's Fee and its costs of carrying out the ESS Activities, including in the event that:

the implementation of the Amended Variation Order and the ESS Variation Proposal does not result in any energy savings or the creation of any Energy Savings Certificates;

the ESC Sale Proceeds are insufficient to cover the ACP's Fee; or

the Net ESC Sale Proceeds are insufficient to cover the Supplier's Fee.

The Supplier agrees that it assumes the full risk of, and will have no claim against Sydney Trains arising out of or in connection with, the implementation of the ESS Variation Proposal and the arrangements contemplated under this Deed.

No Liability for Fees

The parties acknowledge that the ACP's Fee and the Supplier's Fee will be reimbursed from the ESC Sale Proceeds, and Sydney Trains will never be liable to pay the ACP's Fee or the Supplier's Fee, or for any costs incurred by the Supplier in implementing the ESS Variation Proposal (including any fees or other costs incurred by NGE, the M&V Professional and the Scheme Auditor), including in the event that:

the implementation of the Amended Variation Order and the ESS Variation Proposal does not result in any energy savings or the creation of any Energy Savings Certificates;

the ESC Sale Proceeds are insufficient to cover the ACP's Fee; or

the Net ESC Sale Proceeds are insufficient to cover the Supplier's Fee or any costs incurred by the Supplier in implementing the ESS Variation Proposal.

Sydney Trains Share of Proceeds

The Supplier acknowledges that:

the Sydney Trains Share of Proceeds will not be subject to any deduction for fees (including registration fees), costs, charges, expenses or taxes; and

any fees (including registration fees), costs, charges, expenses or taxes incurred by the Supplier, NGE, the M&V Professional or the Scheme Auditor are included in, and accounted for by, the ACP's Fee and the Supplier's Fee.

The Supplier must provide to Sydney Trains any other information reasonably requested by Sydney Trains in connection with the ESS Activities and the implementation of the ESS Variation Proposal.
