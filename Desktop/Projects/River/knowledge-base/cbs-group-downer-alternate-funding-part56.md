SCHEDULE

BACKGROUND

CBS Group are accredited under various Environment Schemes and carries on a business involving capturing, ensuring compliance and submitting information as required for the creation of Environmental Products (Certificates), the trading and sale of the Certificates, and consulting services associated with these activities.

Downer carries on a business that at times involves consulting and advice on  the procurement, design, installation, servicing and/or maintenance of efficient end-user equipment by their Customers which may be entitled for the creation of Environmental Products (Certificates) under the Environmental Schemes.  These are Scheme Activities performed using Approved Products & Services.

CBS Group has agreed to provide Services to Downer and Downer’s Customers to enable funding from Australian Environment Schemes.  CBS Group will work with Downer to collect evidence. Downer will receive payments based on  the successful registration of certificates for the nominated projects (Scheme Activities)
