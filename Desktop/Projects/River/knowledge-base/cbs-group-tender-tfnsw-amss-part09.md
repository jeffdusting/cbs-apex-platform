Price 

CBS Group acknowledges all “Notes to the Respondent”.

Transition-In services

CBS Group anticipates transition in to be completed within 3 business days and will be lead by Kate Heath.   These services are provided at no charge to TfNSW.

Transition-out services

TfNSW may select one (or both) of the following two transition out options:

Option 1: A Traditional transition-out where nominated successors are identified and a transition of knowledge and support is scheduled over 4 weeks.   Costs associated with this option are limited to the CBS Transition Manager who is charged with:

confirming the scope of knowledge and skills to be transitioned

working with TfNSW to confirm the transition recipients for any ongoing works and associated document storage locations

development of a transition guide capturing the location of artefacts and status of works in progress

Option 2: As detailed Alternate Offer 3, creation of an instance of CBS Continuity that provides an enduring chatbot so that future TfNSW can continue to engage with the CBS team knowledge as established at the point of transition out.

Pricing for both options is provided in the following table, noting that in the event that more than one CBS staff member is retained, Transition Out is provided at a 50% cost reduction to the rates shown below.

Discounts and rebates

CBS Group will apply a discount of 2.5% to the escalated rates applied to any new purchase orders of greater than $1m issued. Purchase orders below this threshold do not attract this discount, and the initial fixed fee engagement does not attract this discount. 

Discounts will also apply under the same conditions in the event that Alternate Offer 1 is selected.

Pricing Assumptions

All travel for Melbourne based resources is incorporated into the rates and assuming prior travel requirements and frequencies are sustained.   Any material change in travel requirements will result in an adjustment to the rate or a request for travel costs.
