Control Systems

Change Management and Management of Change (MoC)

In addition to the systems and engineering skills required to manage changes to complex technical and functional systems, CBS brings a unique capability to the requirements to manage changes in culture and organisational dynamics. 

Globally, failed culture change is identified as a root cause of major project failures. CBS bring the value of our experience gained in developing high-performance technical and commercial teams, both client and contractor side to mitigate this risk.

We welcome the opportunity to provide a detailed proposal for cultural change management to support the asset management transition.
