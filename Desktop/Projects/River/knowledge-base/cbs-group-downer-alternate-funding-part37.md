  BACKGROUND

  A.        Wattly is accredited under various Environment Schemes and carries on a business involving the capturing,
            ensuring compliance and submitting information required for the creation of Environmental Products (Certificates),
            the trading and sale of the Certificates, and consulting services associated with these activities.
  B.        The Partner carries on a business involving the consulting and advisory services for the procurement, design,
            installation, servicing and/or maintenance of efficient end-user equipment by their Customers which may be
            entitled for the creation of Environmental Products (Certificates) under the Environmental Schemes. These are
            Scheme Activities performed using Approved Products & Services.
  C.        Wattly has agreed to provide Wattly Services to the Partner and the Partner’s Customers to enable the Customers
            to take advantage of funding from the Environment Schemes. Wattly will work with the Partner’s Customer to
            collect evidence from the Customer. The Partner will need to provide Wattly certain Evidence and may need to
            assist Wattly in obtaining evidence from the Customer. The Partner will receive commission payments based on
            successful registration of certificates for Customer projects (Scheme Activities)
