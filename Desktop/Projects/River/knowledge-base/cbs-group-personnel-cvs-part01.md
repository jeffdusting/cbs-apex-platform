WOL Analysis and Data Specialist 

Qualifications: BEng (Electrical), University of Melbourne  |  MIEAust  |  Leadership Development Program, Melbourne Business School

Professional Profile

David is a senior engineer with 30 years of experience in systems engineering, reliability modelling, operational safety management and data-driven analytics across major infrastructure and transport projects. He brings deep expertise in developing quantitative models that translate complex operational data into actionable insights for decision-makers.

David’s career combines hands-on engineering experience with advanced analytical capabilities, including dynamic risk modelling, reliability analysis and capital expenditure optimisation. Providing systems engineering expertise to ensure consistency and rigour in data management processes

Gethin Perry

Asset Strategy and Engineering Lead

Qualifications: MBA, University of South Wales  |  BEng (Hons), Cardiff University  |  Certified Associate in Asset Management (AMC)

Professional Profile

Gethin is a senior asset management and infrastructure leader with more than 25 years of experience in the development, operation and maintenance of transport and infrastructure assets. He brings extensive expertise in asset lifecycle planning, KPI frameworks, capital investment strategy and stakeholder engagement across complex, multi-asset environments.

Most recently, Gethin led a multidisciplinary team responsible for the major maintenance and long-term capital investment management of 11 toll roads in Sydney, delivering safe and reliable journeys for one million daily customers. His work has consistently focused on translating engineering risk and asset condition data into actionable KPI-based frameworks that enable executive decision-making and drive measurable performance improvements.

Gethin brings direct experience in maturity assessment, benchmarking, portfolio governance and organisational change — capabilities that are central to the successful delivery of the Department of Health’s maintenance KPI implementation program.

Role on This Engagement

As Asset Strategy and Engineering Lead, Gethin will provide overall technical leadership for the engagement, ensuring that the methodology is grounded in best-practice asset management principles and aligned with ISO 55000 and the Victorian AMAF. He will be the primary point of accountability for delivery quality and client outcomes across all five phases.

Key responsibilities on this project include:

Overall technical direction and quality assurance across the five-phase program

Leading the Phase 1 discovery and assessment process, including maturity assessments and readiness reviews of health service maintenance models

Directing the Phase 2 benchmarking methodology, including KPI performance analysis and health service segmentation into maturity cohorts

Overseeing the development of tailored implementation pathways and action plans for each cohort

Providing strategic guidance on governance structures, risk management and continuous improvement mechanisms

Engaging with senior Department stakeholders to ensure alignment, transparency and confidence throughout delivery

He has also led the development and implementation of new business strategies and operating models for organisations across multiple industries.
