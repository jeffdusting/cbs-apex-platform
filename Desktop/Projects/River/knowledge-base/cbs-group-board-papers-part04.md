CobaltBlu’s board comprises two directors — Sarah Margaret Taylor and Jeffrey Alan Dusting — who are married to each other and are both directors of Water Roads. Unlike CBS, CobaltBlu does not have an independent director, and all decisions are made under section 194 of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) (the proprietary company exception).

(a) CBS visibility. As CBS’s independent director, your presence gives CBS direct visibility into how CobaltBlu governs its Water Roads investment — which is relevant given the shared interests and cross-directorships across the three entities.

(b) Governance enhancement. Your presence provides an external perspective at a board that would otherwise operate without any independent voice. While you would have no obligation to intervene, your observations will be recorded in the minutes, creating an additional layer of accountability.

(c) Investor confidence. Series A investors conducting due diligence on the Water Roads cap table and related-party arrangements will see that the CBS independent director had line of sight across all three levels of the structure — Water Roads, CBS, and CobaltBlu. This strengthens the overall conflict management narrative.

(d) You may speak at the meeting if invited to do so by the chair. The agenda includes a standing item (“Observer’s comments”) for you to raise any observations or questions. Your comments will be recorded in the minutes.

(e) Board papers will be provided to you in advance of each meeting, subject to any confidentiality restrictions the board considers appropriate. (f) You may share relevant information from CobaltBlu board meetings with the CBS board to the extent reasonably necessary for CBS to discharge its governance obligations in relation to its Water Roads investment. CobaltBlu acknowledges that this information-sharing is a core purpose of the observer arrangement.

(g) This invitation does not create any right for CBS to appoint an observer or director to the CobaltBlu board. It is a voluntary governance arrangement that exists at CobaltBlu’s pleasure.
