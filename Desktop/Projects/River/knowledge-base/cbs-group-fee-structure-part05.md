## 7. ABOUT
**URL:** /about

### Company Overview

CBS Group is a team of senior, respected consultants delivering strategic and project management, and technical and business advisory services to leading companies across a diverse range of industries. Our people are sought after for their skills, integrity, and performance under pressure.

**Established in 2002**, CBS Group has evolved through strategic growth:
- 2002: Operational Concepts founded
- 2008: SMS Management and Technology partially acquires Operational Concepts
- 2015: CobaltBlu and Sandwood merge to form CBS Group
- 2016: CBS Systems Engineering Practice established
- 2017: CBS Strategy and Technology Practice launched
- 2019: CBS Group acquires Kentville Consulting
- 2022: CBS Technical Advisory Practice established
- 2025: CBS Group acquired by CobaltBlu and Pertain

We have two key practice areas:
- Asset Management & Systems Engineering
- Business & Technology Strategy

CBS's key markets are infrastructure, transport, utilities and telecommunications, with clients from both the commercial and government (Commonwealth and state) sectors. CBS has deep knowledge and experience in road, ITS and rail strategies and projects.

### Our Leadership Team

**Jeff Dusting FIEAust, CPEng**
*Partner, Technical Advisory Services*

Jeff leads the Technical Advisory Practice incorporating our innovation and strategy group and specialist technical services supporting clients in Rail, Roads, Aviation and Defence. His team are engaged in Intelligent Transport Projects (ITS) nationally and internationally, including definition, delivery and strategic planning for next generation tolling, Control Systems, CAVS, safety systems upgrades, communications and signage.

**Sarah Taylor**
*Partner, Business Advisory*

Sarah leads our Business Advisory Service and is Co-Director of Strategy and Technical Innovation. She is nationally recognised for her facilitation services, building high performance consortium and joint venture teams for complex infrastructure projects. Based on senior strategic roles with consulting firms, Quantum Technical Solutions, SMS Management and Technology, and Hewitt Associates, Sarah has a proven ability to communicate complex engineering, construction, technology and project management requirements.

**Jim Ellwood**
*Partner, Systems, Safety and Reliability*

Jim is a senior consulting engineer with over 25 years experience in the analysis and management of highly complex, safety related systems in the utilities, transport, aerospace, defense and infrastructure industries. He has managed and undertaken projects from requirements definition through to delivery, applying and tailoring the systems engineering methodology.

### Our Clients Include
