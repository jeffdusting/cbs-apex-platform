| Budget Re-allocation: Control Systems | Budget Re-allocation: Control Systems |

| Purpose: To seek approval to transfer the budget provisions for Sydney Harbour Tunnel (SHT) and Western Harbour Tunnel (WHT) control systems maintenance and lifecycle refurbishment from Road Maintenance & Resilience (RMR) to Finance, Technology and Commercial (FTC) Operational Technology (OT). | Purpose: To seek approval to transfer the budget provisions for Sydney Harbour Tunnel (SHT) and Western Harbour Tunnel (WHT) control systems maintenance and lifecycle refurbishment from Road Maintenance & Resilience (RMR) to Finance, Technology and Commercial (FTC) Operational Technology (OT). |

| Analysis: The Harbour Crossings Operations and Maintenance Strategy (HCOM Strategy – BN0011347 approved 23 Feb 2025) returned Functional Coordinator responsibility for SHT and WHT control systems to Transport.   The BN also specified an immediate action to assess options for the consolidation of asset management and support of control systems and ITS in the context of current arrangements.
The Functional Coordinator,  asset management and support of SHT and WHT control systems is currently incorporated into the Asset Management contracts with Ventia, with the budget and contract administration provided by RMR.
To minimise risks associated with the development of WHT control system, to simplify decision making regarding the current supportability challenges for the SHT control system,  and ensure control systems do not put WHT opening and TMC tunnel operations program at risk, the transition of Functional Coordinator, asset management and support of SHT and WHT control systems is to FTC OT immediately, with some transitions activities already complete.
The budget for SHT and WHT control systems maintenance and lifecycle works is currently included in RMR budgets.   
To enable streamlined control systems support decision making and appropriate resourcing of the associated activities, this BN seeks approval to effect the immediate transfer of these control system budgets to FTC OT.   The budget to be transferred is the RMR budget provision only, with the works to confirm the adequacy of this budget resting with FTC OT, and the works to reffect the descoping of the WHT SHT AM Contract retained by RMR. 
Critical date: If the briefing note is not approved by the end of September, WHT control system design review activities may not be completed with associated project delivery risks, and the SHT supportability risks will persist with ambiguous accountability for performance. | Analysis: The Harbour Crossings Operations and Maintenance Strategy (HCOM Strategy – BN0011347 approved 23 Feb 2025) returned Functional Coordinator responsibility for SHT and WHT control systems to Transport.
