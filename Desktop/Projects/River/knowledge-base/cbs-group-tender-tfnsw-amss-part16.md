Executive Summary: A Partnership of Certainty, Value, and Commitment

On behalf of the entire CBS Group, I am delighted to present our proposal to continue our valued partnership with Transport for NSW (TfNSW). Over the past 18 months, we have been privileged to work alongside your Asset Management team on some of the state’s most significant infrastructure programs. The trust you have placed in us is a responsibility we take very seriously. This proposal is our commitment to repaying that trust by delivering what we believe no other partner can: the certainty of proven performance, the security of zero transition risk, and a genuine partnership dedicated to achieving the best outcomes for NSW.

Our proposal is built on a foundation of certainty. In a landscape often defined by complexity and unpredictability, CBS Group offers a proven, low-risk path to achieving TfNSW’s strategic asset management objectives. Our submission is a continuation of the measurable success we have already achieved together.

The Certainty of Absolute Alignment with the State’s Best Interests

CBS Group’s greatest strength, and the foundation of our certainty promise, is our unique market position. We ensure that our recommendations serve only the best interests of the State, free from the inherent conflicts and limitations of other advisory options. 

In seeking strategic asset management advice, TfNSW faces a choice between two traditional models, each with significant risks:

The Engineering Design House: While offering analytical rigour, they often suffer from operations-phase blind spots due to limited real-world delivery experience. Their advice can default to “lazy precedent” rather than first-principles thinking, and innovation is frequently constrained by professional indemnity exposure, leading to conservative, risk-averse solutions.

The Asset Management Delivery Provider: While possessing operational experience, their advice is often shaped to suit their own commercial interests and delivery capabilities. This creates a clear conflict of interest—or the perception of one—that can undermine market confidence and compromise the integrity of the procurement process.

CBS Group threads this needle. We provide a third option, combining the best attributes of both models without their respective limitations. We deliver the analytical calibre of a top-tier engineering consultancy, informed by the genuine, hard-won experience of an operations provider. Because we are independent from downstream delivery contracts, our advice is never biased by a competing commercial agenda. We are free to challenge established norms and drive genuine innovation, ensuring our solutions are aligned solely with achieving the best value for the State and its communities.

With CBS, TfNSW can have absolute certainty that our advice is impartial, our analysis is rigorous, and our only goal is your success.

The Certainty of Proven Performance

Our track record with TfNSW speaks for itself. We have delivered over $1.049 billion in validated savings across your programs through the rigorous application of our proprietary CAPITAL Framework. This is not a theoretical model but a proven engine for value creation, demonstrated on your own assets. Our consistent 97.2% on-time delivery rate and a 4.7/5.0 client satisfaction score reflect the strength of our collaborative relationship and our deep understanding of your operational and strategic needs. These are the tangible outcomes of our embedded partnership with you.

The Certainty of Zero Transition Risk

Continuity is a powerful strategic advantage. Our proposal eliminates the significant risks, costs, and delays associated with onboarding a new provider. We offer TfNSW the certainty of immediate, uninterrupted productivity from Day 1 by proposing the a core team of seven senior specialists, most of whom who are already delivering for you. This team, with over 175 years of combined experience, possesses an invaluable understanding of your assets, governance, and priorities. By choosing CBS Group, you avoid a typical 7-12 month learning curve and the associated productivity dip.

The Certainty of a Genuine Partnership and Commitment to NSW

As a 100% Australian-owned SME, our success is intrinsically linked to the prosperity of NSW. We offer an agile, responsive service model that provides direct access to senior leadership, ensuring greater efficiency and value. Our commitment extends beyond the project to creating lasting social and economic benefits for the people of NSW, demonstrated through our First Nations Engineering Internship & Innovation Fund and our dedication to 95%+ local content.

In summary, CBS Group offers TfNSW a partnership built on a foundation of trust and proven results. We provide the certainty of an expert team that is already performing, a methodology that has already delivered exceptional value, and a shared commitment to the future success of Transport for NSW. We look forward to the opportunity to continue this successful journey with you.
