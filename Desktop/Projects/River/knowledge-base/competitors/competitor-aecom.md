## Strengths

- Scale and depth of resources — can staff large concurrent engagements
- Incumbent on major TfNSW and Sydney Metro panels
- Strong brand recognition with government procurement
- Global case study library for benchmarking

## Weaknesses / CBS Advantage

- Generic methodology — lacks CBS's CAPITAL framework with quantified outcome guarantees
- Large firm overhead reflected in pricing — CBS's specialist focus enables competitive pricing
- Personnel rotation — large firms rotate staff; CBS offers named, committed personnel
- Advisory-only positioning on commercial — CBS integrates technical + commercial advisory
- AECOM's AM approach is typically condition-based; CBS's CAPITAL framework is whole-of-life outcome-based
