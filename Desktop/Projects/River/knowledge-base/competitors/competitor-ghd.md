# Competitor Profile — GHD

## Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Company | GHD Group Pty Ltd (private) |
| Headquarters | Sydney, Australia |
| Size | ~10,000 employees globally, ~6,000 in Australia/NZ |
| Key Sectors | Engineering advisory, water, transport, property, mining, energy |
| Geographic Coverage | National + NZ, SE Asia, Americas |

## Capabilities

- Engineering advisory across infrastructure lifecycle
- Water and wastewater infrastructure (strong market position)
- Transport infrastructure advisory and design
- Asset management strategy and implementation
- Advisory services for government and private sector
- Digital advisory and smart infrastructure

## Strengths

- Australian-headquartered — strong government procurement credibility
- Deep Victorian state government relationships
- Broad engineering capability across multiple sectors
- Strong in water and environmental — can offer cross-sector expertise
- Mid-market size — more agile than AECOM/WSP/Jacobs but still significant scale

## Weaknesses / CBS Advantage

- GHD's AM capability is part of their broader engineering practice — CBS is AM-specialist with CAPITAL framework
- Less focused on transport infrastructure operations and tolling — CBS has niche expertise in motorway AM
- GHD's advisory tends toward traditional engineering approaches — CBS brings commercial integration
- GHD's Victorian strength means less focus on NSW transport (CBS's primary market)

## Win Themes Against GHD

1. "CAPITAL framework is purpose-built for infrastructure AM with quantified outcomes — GHD applies general engineering advisory methodology"
2. "CBS's deep NSW transport relationships (TfNSW, SHT, WHT, M6) — specific client knowledge and performance history"
3. "Specialist tolling and motorway operations expertise — CBS's niche focus vs GHD's generalist engineering approach"
