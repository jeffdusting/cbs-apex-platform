## Strengths

- Deep tunnelling expertise from design through operations
- Strong in systems engineering and complex infrastructure
- Large project delivery capability (design JVs, PMO roles)
- International case studies in tunnel operations
- Defence sector clearances and capability

## Weaknesses / CBS Advantage

- Jacobs focuses on technical design excellence — CBS differentiates with commercial integration (CAPITAL framework combines technical AM with value-based commercial outcomes)
- Large firm pricing — Jacobs' overhead structure typical of major engineering consultancies
- Less focus on whole-of-life commercial optimisation — CBS's CAPITAL framework specifically targets cost reduction and value enhancement
- Jacobs' AM approach tends toward asset condition assessment; CBS adds lifecycle commercial modelling
