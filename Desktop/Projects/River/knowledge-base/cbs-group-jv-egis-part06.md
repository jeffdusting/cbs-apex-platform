SPECIALIST TECHNICAL AND ADVISORY SERVICES (TASP) PANEL – INDEXATION OF RATES

I refer to the Specialist and Technical Advisory Services Panel Conditions of Contract between Egis Oceania and
Department of Transport (DoT), dated 13 June 2019. In that contract, Schedule 13 (Indexation of Rates) states
that:
          “(a). Effective from 1 July immediately following the Commencement Date, the Rates will be adjusted
          annually in accordance with the following formula: Adjusted amount = Payment Multiplier X Original
          Amount

           Where: Payment Multiplier – relevant Wage Price Index (Private Sector) published by the Australian
           Bureau of Statistics immediately before the relevant 1 July divided by the Wage Price Index for the
           equivalent quarter in the previous year; and

           Original Amount – the amount (whether previously adjusted or not) applicable immediately prior to the
           relevant 1 July (disregarding rounding (if any) applied to that at the previous 1 July). Provided that, where
           the Payment Multiplier results in a figure less than 1.0, the Payment Multiplier will be 1.0.”
