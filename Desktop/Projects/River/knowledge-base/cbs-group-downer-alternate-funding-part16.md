### Slide 2
Summary & Context
Executive Summary
Federal and State Government, and private schemes provide financial support for initiatives that reduce CO2 emissions (directly or as energy savings).
The schemes are a potential source of competitive advantage to many Downer transport and infrastructure projects, and maintenance / support contracts.
This source of funds is connected to the environmental value of initiative, and not connected to the project costThe value of funds available varies with the scheme and the size / nature of the project.
Recommendations:
Mobilise to secure funds for the fleet HVAC Project now
Develop a ‘Go-NoGo’ framework for all Downer projects / bids that incorporates a set of rules to select the right projects and schemes
Engagement Context
CBS were engaged to assess the potential for Downer to access State and Federal government funding to support a range of current and planned initiatives
For this analysis, we drill down on the 2 schemes most applicable to these circumstances ( HVAC - Fleet) and assume that the priority is to receive the funds upfront.

### Slide 20
Conclusions and Recommendation
Conclusion 1
The NSW ESS Scheme, using the Analysis & Verification methodology is most appropriate for the Sydney Fleet HVAC Initiative, providing up to $7.5m in funding in advance of installation.
Funding is an incentive to reduce energy consumption and independent of project cost.
Recommendation 1Define the ‘tranches’ and scope of modifications (HVAC and others TBC) so optimize NSW ESS funds and alignment with the project implementation schedule
Recommendation 2Brief TfNSW to inform of this proposed action
Recommendation 3Engage CBS Group to support the application process, including the services of an approved auditor to review & refine the plan
Conclusion 2
Energy / CO2 Reduction incentive schemes represent a significant point of difference for Downer, providing a supplemental source of revenue and capital for a range of projects.
The most appropriate scheme varies with jurisdiction, project type and size.
Recommendation 4Develop a ‘Go-NoGo’ framework for all Downer projects / bids that incorporates a set of rules to select the right projects and schemes
Recommendation 5Engage CBS Group to provide briefings to selected executives and leaders so that the potential application of these schemes is understood widely.
