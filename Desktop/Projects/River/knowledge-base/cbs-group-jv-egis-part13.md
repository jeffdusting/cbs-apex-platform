Risk Management

We understand that successful project delivery requires effective risk management, prompt issue resolution, and the identification of opportunities for continuous improvement. With our extensive experience in managing complex projects, including infrastructure and tolling systems across Australia and the Asia-Pacific region, we bring a risk-based approach (RBA) to every stage of the project, as we have on similar projects.

Our approach includes identifying and quantifying risks and issues across relevant disciplines and phases and include design, construction, testing, commissioning, operations, market, network, industrial relations, and asset ownership. We follow the AS/NZS 31000:2018 Risk Management framework, ensuring that management processes are structured and effective.

Risk and issue management includes managing risks inherent in delivering services and importantly, ensuring that the processes are in place to adequately solicit and manage to conclusion, the client’s and Contractors’ risks which could impact successful project delivery.

Culture of Sharing Knowledge

We understand that knowledge transfer and continuity are critical to the long-term success of renewable energy in Australia. Our team's deep experience working in this industry ensures a smooth integration of our advisory services with their operations.
