Executive summary

CBS Group proposes an innovative Value-Based Partnership (VBP) framework that aligns our success directly with Transport for NSW's (TfNSW) operational improvements and benefit realisation. This proposal presents a shared-risk commercial model where CBS Group accepts below-market initial fees in exchange for performance-based rewards tied to independently validated benefits.

Through this partnership, we address critical challenges in infrastructure asset management including standards optimisation, digital transformation, operational excellence, and sustainable innovation. The framework positions CBS Group as the prime contractor with single-point accountability, with the option to leverage specialist expertise from ST Engineering for global benchmarking and validation, and Resolve Group for operations support, when appropriate to add value.

Our commitment to this partnership is demonstrated through our willingness to invest alongside TfNSW, accepting reduced initial compensation while focusing on generating measurable, sustainable improvements that benefit both parties. This arrangement ensures our complete alignment with TfNSW's strategic objectives and operational success.
