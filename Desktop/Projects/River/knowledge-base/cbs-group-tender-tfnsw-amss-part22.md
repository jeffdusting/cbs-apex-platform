1.         Introduction: Our Commitment to Ethical Excellence
At CBS Group, our mission is to transform infrastructure performance. We achieve this by adhering to our core
ethos: Do Good, For Good, With Good People. This Code of Conduct serves as our guide, providing a
framework for ethical decision-making and professional behaviour in all our interactions. It applies to every
member of the CBS team, including employees, contractors, and partners, ensuring we uphold the highest
standards of integrity and excellence. This Code is informed by principles of critical thinking, peer review, and a
commitment to delivering ethical outcomes that create lasting value for our clients, our communities, and our
company.

2.         Our Guiding Principles
Our ethos is the foundation of our Code of Conduct, shaping how we approach our work, our relationships,
and our impact on the world.

Do Good: Excellence in Every Engagement
We are committed to delivering work of the highest quality. This means applying rigorous analysis, innovative
thinking, and our deep industry expertise to every challenge. We embrace a culture of critical thinking,
constantly questioning assumptions and seeking the most effective solutions. Our systems thinking approach
ensures we consider all facets of a problem to deliver measurable, tangible results that stand up to scrutiny.

For Good: Infrastructure That Serves Australia
Our work has a profound impact on the world around us. We are driven by a sense of purpose to ensure that
infrastructure investments deliver not just financial returns, but also social, environmental, and community
benefits. We are committed to acting in the public interest, promoting sustainability, and using frameworks like
our Catalyst 7 methodology to create multi-dimensional, ethical outcomes.

With Good People: The Power of True Partnership
We believe that the best outcomes are achieved through collaboration and respect. We foster a culture of peer
review, where constructive feedback and diverse perspectives are valued. We build trust with our clients,
partners, and colleagues by being transparent, accountable, and working as a unified team. We are committed
to creating a safe, inclusive, and supportive work environment where everyone can thrive.

4.          Ethical Decision-Making Framework
When faced with an ethical dilemma, this framework, rooted in critical thinking and peer review, will guide you:

1. Assess: Is this action aligned with our core ethos of "Do Good, For Good, With Good People"?
2. Scrutinise: Would this decision withstand public scrutiny and the review of my peers?
3. Consider: Have I considered the potential impact on all stakeholders, including our clients, the community,
   and the environment?
4. Consult: Have I sought advice from my manager or a trusted colleague to gain a different perspective?



5.          Speaking Up
We are committed to an open and transparent culture where everyone feels safe to raise concerns without fear
of retaliation. If you witness or suspect any violation of this Code, you are encouraged to report it to your
manager, a member of the leadership team, or through our designated confidential reporting channels.



6.          Conclusion
This Code of Conduct is more than a document; it is a reflection of who we are and how we operate. By
embracing these principles, we will continue to build a company respected for its expertise, integrity, and
commitment to making a positive impact on the world. We are all responsible for upholding this Code and
ensuring that CBS Group remains a leader in infrastructure advisory and a force for good.
