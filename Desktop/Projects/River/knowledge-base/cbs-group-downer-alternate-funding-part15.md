1   Federal Scheme: Energy Reduction Fund (ERF)
    Reverse Auction of credits earned in arrears



Description:
• Carbon Credits are awarded per tonne of CO2e stored/avoided by the project
• Project ‘life’ of 7 years
• Credits can be sold via abatement contracts or on the secondary market

Advantages:
• Comprehensive calculation method [limited ambiguity]
• Does not require additional measuring devices

Disadvantages:
• No guarantee of value – reverse auction
• No upfront funding
• Legislated until 2025 only
