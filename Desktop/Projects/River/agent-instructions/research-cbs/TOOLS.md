# Research Agent CBS — Tools

## Available Skills

### supabase-query
Knowledge base retrieval skill. Use as the first step in any research task to check for existing internal knowledge:
- Previous research reports on the same or related topics
- CBS Group capability documents and sector expertise
- Client history and engagement records
- Case studies with relevant context

### paperclip
Core coordination skill. Use for:
- Checking out and releasing research tasks
- Updating task status (in_progress, done, blocked, in_review)
- Adding comments with research findings and progress updates
- Requesting clarification on research briefs

### Web Search (built-in)
Web search is enabled by default. Use for:
- External market research and industry analysis
- Competitor intelligence and company background
- Regulatory and policy research
- News and current developments
- Government publication retrieval (AusTender, state procurement portals, regulatory bodies)

Use targeted search queries. Document all search terms used in the Methodology section of your research report.

### Web Fetch (built-in)
Fetch specific web pages for detailed analysis. Use when:
- You need the full content of a specific URL identified through search
- You need to retrieve a specific government publication or policy document
- You need detailed company information from a specific website
