## Scope of Work Summary

Transport for NSW seeks a suitably qualified consultancy to provide Asset Management Support Services across its road and transport infrastructure portfolio. The engagement covers:

- Development and maintenance of whole-of-life cost models for major transport assets
- Asset management strategy, planning, and performance frameworks
- Technical and commercial due diligence for transport concession assets
- Systems engineering assurance and independent verification
- Tolling system lifecycle management and optimisation
- Asset commissioning, transition planning, and end-of-contract support
- 30-year asset management models and lifecycle costings

The scope spans multiple TfNSW-managed assets including motorways (WestConnex M4, M5, M8), Sydney Harbour Tunnel, and M6, covering both operational assets and those in construction-to-operations transition.

---

## Key Requirements

1. **Technical Capability:** Demonstrated experience in infrastructure asset management, systems engineering, and tolling advisory services across road and transport assets.

2. **Commercial Framework:** Ability to offer both a conforming fixed-fee proposal and alternative value-based pricing models that align consultant compensation with measurable outcomes.

3. **Standards Compliance:** Working knowledge of ISO 55001 (Asset Management), ISO 44001 (Collaborative Business Relationships), and relevant Australian/NZ transport standards.

4. **Key Personnel:** Named team with relevant qualifications and demonstrable experience on comparable engagements (minimum: engagement lead, technical lead, commercial lead).

5. **Methodology:** Clear articulation of the consultant's proprietary or adopted asset management methodology, including how it integrates with TfNSW's existing asset management framework.

6. **Condition of Participation:** Respondents must demonstrate specific experience in video-only tolling models, whole-of-life costings for major motorway assets, and asset commissioning/transition on projects of comparable scale.

7. **Information Security:** Compliance with TfNSW information security requirements, including data handling, access controls, and personnel security clearances as applicable.
